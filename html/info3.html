General-purpose photovoltaic device model
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~
https://www.gpvdm.com

To make a new simulation click New Simulation in the menu or to open an existing simulation select Open simulation.

There is more help on the man pages.
Please report bugs to me by e-mailing: info@gpvdm.com.
